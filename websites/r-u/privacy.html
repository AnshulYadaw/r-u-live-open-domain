Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2023


Information Collection

R-U Live is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect or store the URLs you check on our servers
  • Your check history is stored only in your browser's local storage
  • No personal information is collected or transmitted

Local Storage

This site uses browser local storage to save your recent check history. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.


Analytics

We use basic, privacy-focused analytics to collect anonymous usage information, such as:

  • Number of visitors to the site
  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on site

This information helps us improve the service but cannot be used to identify you.


Third Parties

When you check a website status, a request is made to that website's server. We do not control those websites or their privacy practices.


Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.


Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@r-u.live.
